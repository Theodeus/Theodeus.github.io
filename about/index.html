Hello!

I'm an Interactive Developer and Technical Director.

I'm currently working at Electronic Arts (DICE). I have previously worked at Goodbye Kansas, North Kingdom and DinahMoe. I also spent a year consulting at the Swedish Transport Administration, for good measure.

I have extensive experience in interactive development and a specialization in adaptive music and audio. I've worked with motion capture, VR/AR, photogrammetry, voice controlled applications, non-linear video, online jamming, 2D & 3D games and creative concept & writing. And classical frontend development, of course.

In doing so, I've won a bunch of awards, including 7 Adobe Cutting Edge Award, 5 FWA Site of Month, 42 FWA Site/Mobile of the Day, a Red Dot Award, Cannes Lions and Awwwards of different pedigrees.

I equally enjoy people and writing code. I consider myself tech agnostic and have worked with a wide range of tech stacks, though I tend to go with full stack JavaScript or Unity for my private projects.
